About Us

We are Team Vespera — a group of passionate students dedicated to exploring the cosmos and uncovering the mysteries of exoplanets. Our mission is to harness the power of artificial intelligence and modern data science to identify and classify exoplanets, making space exploration more accessible and data-driven for everyone.

Our team brings together students with diverse backgrounds in computer science, and data analysis. Together, we developed a machine learning model capable of detecting exoplanets by analyzing real astronomical data — a project that merges our curiosity about the universe with our technical skills.

At Vespera, we value curiosity, precision, and the pursuit of knowledge. Our work reflects our commitment to learning, experimentation, and contributing to the broader understanding of worlds beyond our own.

The goal of our model and platform is to provide a practical tool for researchers and enthusiasts to identify potential exoplanets more efficiently, with a special focus on discovering new planets that could be habitable. By sharing our work, we hope to support ongoing scientific discovery and open new paths for exploring the vast expanse of our universe.
